Who I am

01 About

I am currently a PhD student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at NC State University. I'm supervised by Prof. Nuria González-Prelcic, the director of the Wireless Sensing and Communication Lab (WiSeCom), and work on sensor fusion and machine learning based wireless communications for automated vehicles.

Before this I received an M.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Texas at Austin. I worked in the Wireless Systems Innovation Laboratory (WSIL), supervised by Prof. Robert W. Heath Jr. My research topics were UAV navigation systems based on deep learning, sensor fusion, etc.
